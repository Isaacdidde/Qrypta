Everything You Need to Secure Access

Every feature is designed around zero-trust principles, auditability, and long-term operational safety.

🔐

Zero-Knowledge Encryption

Secrets are encrypted on the client before storage. Qrypta never has access to plaintext data — ever.

🧠

Master Password Isolation

The master key never leaves the user context. Even administrators cannot decrypt vault data.

👥

Role-Based Access Control

Fine-grained permissions tied to roles, departments, and organizational hierarchy.

🧾

Immutable Audit Logs

Every action is logged with timestamps, identity context, and integrity guarantees.

🔄

Soft Delete & Recovery

Accidental deletion never means permanent loss. Restore vault items with full traceability.

🛡️

Least Privilege Enforcement

Access is always minimal by default, reducing blast radius from mistakes or compromise.

📂

Vault Segmentation

Separate credentials by project, team, or department without duplication.

📨

Secure Invitations

Controlled onboarding with role assignment before access is ever granted.

⚙️

Policy-Driven Security

Enforce organization-wide rules for access, approvals, and credential usage.
